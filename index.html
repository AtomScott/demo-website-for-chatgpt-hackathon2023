About the Hackathon

The ChatGPT Hackathon in Tokyo is a two-day event focused on exploring the possibilities of ChatGPT and creating innovative solutions. Participants will have the opportunity to work on creative side projects, tackle innovative challenges, build intellectual chatbots, and create captivating generative art pieces. Join us to shape the future of AI!

Schedule

The event will take place on a weekend in late April or early May. The registration deadline is two weeks before the event. More detailed information about the schedule will be available soon.

Mentors & Speakers

We are working on assembling an outstanding lineup of mentors and speakers for the ChatGPT Hackathon. These experts will guide and inspire participants throughout the event. Stay tuned for more information on our mentors and speakers!
